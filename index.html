Graduate Association of Geography and the Environment

Peer-led Analysis and Cartography in GIS

Welcome!

This is the official website for the Spring 2018 Peer-led Seminar in GIS Analysis and Cartography at UT Austin.

The goal for this class is to provide practical experience GIS and cartographic experience using both proprietary and open source platforms.

The class will meet on Fridays from either 11:00 am to 12:00 pm or 12:00 pm 1:00 pm in CLA 1.402, with 30 minutes after class for optional extended exercises. An email will be sent out each week to confirm the time.
